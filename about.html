About Us

Our Mission

By creating Nota, we want to make your time in college a little easier.

Nota is a simple platform for sharing lecture notes, videos, and other study material. Notes on Nota are taggable and searchable through a simple UI. Upon signing in, you can upload and share your own study material. Nota allows you to tag, organize and search through your lecture notes. Use Nota to study for classes you are enrolled in or to inspect and compare classes you are considering signing up for.
